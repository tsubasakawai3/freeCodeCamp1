Requirements
・On click, toggle switch moves from one side to another
・Cursor becomes a pointer
・Match styles
・No JavaScript!

Hints and Help:
・If you hide the checkbox, you'll still be able to style and
  toggle back and fourth between input:checked and input:unchecked
  pseudo classes by clicking on the checkbox label
・Use a sibling selector to style the .toggle-switch class when the input
  is :checked or :unchecked
